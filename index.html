THE ART OF PROFESSIONAL GEL APPLICATION

Mastering gel nail application requires understanding the chemistry, mastering the technique, and selecting the right tools. This comprehensive guide covers everything a professional needs to know.

THE SCIENCE OF GEL CHEMISTRY

Modern gel systems use a photoinitiator-based chemistry that responds to specific wavelengths of UV or LED light. Understanding this process is fundamental to achieving consistent, long-lasting results. The curing reaction â€” photopolymerization â€” transforms the liquid monomer into a solid polymer network with remarkable durability.

BAEHR's gel formulations are engineered to achieve an optimal balance between flexibility and hardness, ensuring that nails can withstand daily wear without lifting or cracking. Our proprietary bonding technology creates adhesion at the molecular level.

PREPARATION IS EVERYTHING

Proper nail preparation accounts for 70% of a successful gel application. Starting with clean, dehydrated, and properly shaped nails ensures maximum adhesion and longevity.

Use BAEHR's professional nail primer after dehydration for optimal results on all nail types.

Professional Tip: Always cure each layer for the full recommended time. Under-curing is the number one cause of gel lifting and can lead to allergic sensitization over time.

SELECTING THE RIGHT TOOLS

Your tools are an extension of your expertise. Investing in professional-grade equipment from BAEHR ensures precision, hygiene, and consistent results across every client. Our nail drill systems offer variable torque control for different nail types and techniques.

When selecting a nail lamp, consider wattage, wavelength coverage, and timer functionality. BAEHR's 48W LED system covers the full spectrum required for all professional gel brands.
